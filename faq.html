Frequently Asked Question's


1. How do I make a reservation?

You can make a reservation through our website or by calling our restaurant directly.

2. Are their any extra charges for making the reservation?

Yes, the process of reservation is chargeable.

3. Can I modify my reservation?

Yes, you can modify your reservation by contacting us directly.

4. Do you accept walk-ins?

Yes, we welcome walk-ins, but reservations are recommended during peak hours.

5. What is the cancellation policy?

You can cancel your reservation up to 24 hours in advance without any charge.

6. How far in advance can i make a reservation?

You can make a reservation upto 3 months in advance.
